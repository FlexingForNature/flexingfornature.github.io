ABOUT US

Flexing for Nature is a website depicting the authors' journey to a healthy flexitarian diet either for health, beauty, well being, animals and nature.

The name of the website, Flexing for Nature, got its name from the term Flexitarian which is from the words flexible and vegetarian.

A flexitarian is one whose normally meatless diet occasionally includes meat or fish but in the authors' case, they will try to limit dairies too.
