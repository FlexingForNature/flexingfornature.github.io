CHEAP VEGAN SHAWARMA

Cheap, Easy, Healthy, Delicious all in one.
Can be your ideal comfort food!

FOR ONLY 1 SHAWARMA, YOU'LL NEED:

1 Tortilla (Click here to know how)
4 slices of Tomato
4 thin slices of Cucumber
1 leaf of Chinese Cabbage (not the outer leaves)
2 thin slices of Green onion
1/8 cup Button Mushrooms - each sliced into four
1/8 can Black Beans
1/8 cup Corn Kernels
Vegan Mayo
Vegan Cheese

The Healthy Wraps

Shawarma is a dish in the Middle Eastern cuisine. It has meat that is cut into thin slices, stacked into a cone-like shape, and roasted on a slowly-turning vertical rotisserie. It was originally made of lamb or mutton but now, shawarma is also made of chicken, turkey, beef, or veal. It is one of the most healthiest foods across many cuisines. In fact, chicken shawarma only has 475 calories and 83% of it is protein.

But now, we are going to make a healthy shawarma out of only plant proteins and nutrients to make it cleaner and healthier.

  1. STEP 1 - Over high heat, pour the canned black beans and mushrooms on the pan. The canned black beans' water will serve as the water and additional taste of the mushroom so there's no need to add more water unless it tastes too salty. Set aside when done.
  2. STEP 2 - If you're only going to use a normal pan, coat it with only a tablespoon of cooking oil then put the tortilla on it.
  3. STEP 3 - Put 1 leaf of chinese cabbage on the tortilla first then on the cabbage, put the two thin slices of green onion, then put 4 slices of tomato and 4 slices of cucumber. Pour the mushroom-black beans on them and sprinkle the corn kernels. Top them with zigzags of vegan mayonnaise and grated vegan cheese.
  4. STEP 4 - Turn off the stove and using flat tongs, take the shawarma and roll it. If it is hard to roll, use a cooking thread to make it stay in a roll or put it in a shawarma bag or wrap it around a thick tissue or clean paper. Enjoy!
