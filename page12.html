TOFU-CASHEW-BROCCOLI STIR-FRY

Great for a family dinner!
Featuring High Sources of Certain Vitamins and Minerals!

INGREDIENTS

For Rice/Grain:
1 cup of rice or any substitute grain you want
vegetable broth
soy sauce – to taste


For Tofu:
1 block or 350 grams of tofu, cubed
1 ½ tbsp corn starch
¼ tsp sea salt
¼ tsp garlic powder
Vegetable oil for frying


For Cashew-Broccoli:
1 ½ tbsp Brown Sugar
1 tbsp Hot Water
2 tbsp Soy Sauce
½ tsp Lemon Juice
2 tbsp Vegan Butter
4 cups Broccoli Florets
½ cup finely chopped or halved raw cashews
3 cloves Garlic, minced

The Savior Food

I love this combination because broccoli is packed with high amounts of iron and vitamin C while tofu has all the nine essential amino acids so it is a good source of protein and calcium, too. Cashew nuts are also rich in iron and other certain minerals. Cashew nuts can boost your mind and it also contains the good monounsaturated and polyunsaturated fats which are said to help prevent heart disease and stroke.

This food is greatly recommended for students and workers who can hardly take care of themselves due to their very busy schedules.

  1. STEP 1 - For Rice or any substitute grain: Cook it according to how it should be cooked but replace every cup of water required with 1 cup of vegetable broth and 1 tsp soy sauce. 
  2. STEP 2 - For tofu: In a big bowl, combine cubed tofu, corn starch, salt and garlic powder. Mix them gently; be careful not to tear the tofu. 

  3. STEP 3 - Then heat oil in a frying pan. Add the coated tofu and fry. Stir often, until the cubes are evenly golden brown and crispy on all sides.

  4. STEP 4 - For the Cashew-Broccoli: First, stir brown sugar and hot water together until sugar is dissolved. Add soy sauce and lemon juice then set them aside.
  5. STEP 5 - Then on the same pan, heat 1 ½ tbsp vegan butter over high heat. Add the broccoli florets and stir-fry uncovered for two to three minutes. Cover and cook for another 2-3 minutes until they are nearly tender.
  6. STEP 6 - Add another ½ tbsp vegan butter, cashews, and minced garlic. Stir well and cook for another 1-2 minutes, until garlic is cooked and fragnant.
  7. STEP 7 - After that, we can now add the tofu and brown sugar. Mix well and cook for another 1-2 minutes until the sauce is reduced.
  8. STEP 8 - Serve it over your grains then enjoy!
