Different Kinds of Vegetarians

Mainly, vegetarianism is a practice of a meatless diet usually for fitness and health. However, there are different kinds of vegetarians based on their preferred diet specifications.
Lacto-ovo Vegetarians are vegetarians who consume dairy and egg products. They usually follow this kind of diet because they believe that dairies and egg products contain high amounts of essential proteins.
Lacto-Vegetarians are vegetarians who consume dairy but not egg products. This kind of diet is usually followed by vegetarians who have egg allergies or anti-egg beliefs.
Ovo-Vegetarians are vegetarians who consume egg but not dairy products. This kind of diet is usually followed by vegetarians who are lactose intolerant.
Flexitarians are vegetarians who occasionally consume animal products but tries to follow a plant-based diet as much as possible.
Vegans are vegetarians who does not eat any animal products or any products that relates to animal harm or animal cruelty. They usually follow this kind of diet and lifestyle for their pro-animal beliefs and philosophies.
How about you? What kind of vegetarian are you?
