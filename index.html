Why Go Flexitarian?

Flexitarian are from the words flexible and vegetarian. If you want to go vegetarian or vegan but you occasionally can't avoid eating meat, then you can be flexitarian.

1

For Skin

In case you want to help your skin, mainly eating plant-based whole food can give you a detox.

2

For Health

In case you want to get rid of unhealthy fats and protect yourself from certain diseases such as diabetes, this can help you.                                

3

For Animals and Nature

In case you love animals so much that you wanted to stop using any but you can't because of certain stuff like your family does not support it or there are times that you have less time to prepare.
